The UK data for 2007-2013 consists of one dataset that includes: Wales, Northern Ireland, Scotland, England. The 2014-2020 data was collected and covers all funds for England, Northern Ireland and Wales. Amounts are originally in British Pounds, but we converted them to Euro in the additional variable amount, which is depicted here. Due to the data originally being from different sources, the amount variables differ between the countries, so aggregation does not add up to the EU's metadata.
