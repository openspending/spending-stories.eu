For the Czech Republic, the ERDF data could be downloaded in Excel format. Data for both the 2007-2013 and the 2014-2020 period is available, however, the ESF data could not be located. Amounts are originally in Czech Krona, but we converted them to Euro in the additional variable amount, which is depicted here. Beware that the Czech data diverges largely from the EU's data, we assume this is due to third party financing being included.
